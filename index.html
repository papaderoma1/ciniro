Hebrews 13:16

Do not forget to do good and to share with others, for with such sacrifices God is pleased.
This is our motto and that's what we try to fulfil to our best,We are a Faith-Based Non-Profit Organisation. The association's purpose is giving spiritual,material and social assitance to the needy!
Our association came to life on the 24th of September 2001 and since then we have grown alot only through Faith in God, ever since, we are actively doing charity works.
The Association's Registration number is 3437/2001

Want to help?

Our association is aimed to help those in difficult situations

Good can and will conquer the world!

For God called you to do good, even if it means suffering, just as Christ suffered for you. He is your example, and you must follow in his steps.
1 Peter 2:21

Our goals:
- Creating a rehabilitation center for persons suffering from all kind of addictions or those with a broken life.
- Providing shelter for those who don't have one.
- Visiting and preaching in the prisons, helping the inmates to reintegrate in the society upon their release.
- Evangelism by all means; Pro-life, pro-family and other immoral practices activism; Meetings and conferences.
- Building of orphan centres to provide shelter, education, social integration ,according to Christian practices, for the orphans.
- Building a Christian Kindergarten, social canteen, homecare activities, helping the poor etc.

All the above will be done according the the Christian Standards, Romanian Laws and the U.N.O conventions regarding children and human rights.
